WELCOME TO THE FACULTY OF INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY

The Faculty of Information Technology is one of Western University's five faculties, and it is the first of its type in Sri Lanka's national university system. The Faculty of Information Technology (IT) was founded in June 2001 to meet the country's rising demand for IT experts. The Faculty now offers two internal degree programs, Bachelor of Science Honours (B.Sc. Hons.) in Information Technology and Bachelor of Science Honours (B.Sc. Hons.) in Information Technology and Management, as well as an external degree program, Bachelor of Information Technology. In addition, beginning in early 2004, the Faculty will offer a Master of Science in Information Technology, a Master of Science in Artificial Intelligence, a Master of Philosophy, and a Doctoral degree as postgraduate programs. A few professional development programs have been created to meet the urgent demands of the IT sector in the area.
