WELCOME TO THE FACULTY OF TECHNOLOGY

One of the newest faculties to join the famous Western University is the Faculty of Technology. In response to the establishment of the "Technology Stream" for Advanced Level curriculum, Western University took on the issue of accepting students from this stream who met the university's admissions requirements into a new Faculty, the Faculty of Technology. Western University is the country's oldest and highest-ranked higher educational institution, and the new Faculty will represent a significant step forward in the university's quest for excellence in a variety of subjects. Students from diverse fields study at Western University's other seven faculties, institutes, and schools, and the university will guarantee that the students of the new Faculty of Technology have unique opportunity to be among the best.
