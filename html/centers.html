Colombo Center

Powering Great Minds

The Colombo branch was established in 1968.Since its establishment, we have amied at achieving excellence thriugh the programmes it offers to its clients drawn from both public and private sectors of Sri Lanks, as well as those from overseas countries who availed themselves of its services.

Calibrating the future of southerners

The deep South is no longer the only hub of tradition blende with spice or curd an treacle. It is the knowledge hub of the youth and adult professionals catering to the Sri Lankan needs and contexts seasoned with skills to win global challenges.
