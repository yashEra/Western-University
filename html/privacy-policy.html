Privacy Policy

he Western University’s Quality Policy is as follows. Western University will satisfy our consumers by providing high-quality degree, higher diploma, diploma, and certificate programs that fulfill national and international standards, as well as academic and corporate sector-specific criteria and expectations. We maintain a quality management system that complies with ISO 9001:2015 international standards in order to achieve this. We pledge to do the following in order to meet our customers' expectations: Exercise leadership in the provision of high-quality, skills-based higher education. Meet the highest level of service quality and quickness at the most affordable price. Continue to improve and reinvent our products and services. We shall do the following in order to meet the aforementioned goals: Set and implement SMART goals to track and improve our marketing, customer service, enrollments, course design and development, academic support, academic delivery, library, examinations, and awarding processes against worldwide academic standards. Ascertain that the infrastructure and learning environment are compatible with cutting–edge technologies. Align ourselves with the industry's most capable Consultants, who are both academically and professionally certified. Design and administer well-structured training programs to help all of our academic and nonacademic staff improve their skills. Maintain our organization's capability by responding quickly to all risks and opportunities that develop within and outside our business, as well as among our key stakeholders. Continuously develop and maintain the effectiveness of our Quality Management System (CMS).
