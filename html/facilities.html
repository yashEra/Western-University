OUR FACILITIES

Our main campus in Colombo, as well as our regional centers in Kandy and Matara, ensure that quality education is available to a broader range and a greater number of our valued students through Western University. Western University looks forward with excitement, hope, and optimism, and invites you to develop your skills and be a part of our success story.

LIBRARIES

Over 23000 volumes, 400 CD ROMs, instructive video tapes, and chosen periodicals are well-stocked in the fully-fledged library documentation center. In addition to the university's main library, the FCMS has established its own library and subscribes to academic publications in relevant subjects to aid student learning and provide a conducive environment for research and development. For its students, the FCMS also maintains an e-Reference Library, which houses a vast number of reference books and research papers.

LABORATORY

In order to assist teaching and practical activities, we provide a comprehensive IT infrastructure. Students can improve their abilities in a variety of labs. These air-conditioned laboratories are fully equipped. Students are expected to use laboratories according to the timetables provided, and resource usage should adhere to Western University policy.

CAFETERIA

Students have access to a separate canteen area. Students also have access to indoor games such as carrom, a TV lounge, and areas for eating and relaxing. With a focus on our students' health and nutrition, the Western University cafeteria provides a variety of traditional Sri Lankan dishes as well as any type of food and drink at a reasonable price.

HOSTELS

Students should check the Academic Affairs Division's notice boards for accommodation notices or go to Western University's Student Services Reception on level 1 of the main building. With full-time access to students, a dedicated warden and an academic warden are on-site. Pillows, blankets, and bed sheets are among the personal items that students must bring with them when living away from home for an extended period of time.
