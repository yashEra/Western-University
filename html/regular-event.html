REGULAR EVENTS IN THE UNIVERSITY YEAR

The accademic year at University runs from Junary to December the year is divided into semesters at each semester has 15 weeks & end of the semester students have face the final exams. There are some extra curricular activites orgaized by the each faculties to upgrading the students capabilities.




GENERAL GRADUATION

121 General convercation will be held on 01 st of March 2022.

INTER-FACULTY CRICKET MATCH

Inter-Faculty criclcet carnivel organized by the department of sports science to build the friendliness amoung undergraduates. This carnival is to be held on mid of the first semester in the acadamic year.

ANNUAL DEBATE COMPETITION

Annual debate competition is organized by faculty of commerce management studies. The main purpose of this is to build the undergraduates debateing & presenting skills.

Inter University cultural festival is orgranized by the arts association of the University The main objective is to orgaize this event is to given an understanding the historical cultural value to the undergraduates in Sri Lanka.
